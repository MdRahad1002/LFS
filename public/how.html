THE PLAYBOOK

Six steps from zero to jackpot. Simple to learn, impossible to stop playing.

1
💰 Deposit Crypto
Fund your account using BTCETHSOLUSDTBNB. Your crypto is converted into Flag Credits (FC) — the in-game currency. 1 FC = $1 USD equivalent. First deposit gets a +20% bonus. Minimum deposit $10.
2
🌍 Claim Your Territory
Browse the live world map. Each territory costs 10 FC to enter. You can own multiple territories — the more you own, the higher your jackpot share. Each territory you buy adds 9.7 FC to the prize pool (3% house cut). Multiple players can hold the same territory and split winnings.
3
⏱️ The Elimination Clock
Every 60 minutes, the system selects one territory for elimination using a provably fair on-chain random function. All players holding that territory are knocked out — their credits are gone. The map shrinks. The jackpot grows. Tension builds with every tick.
4
📈 Trade the Market
Territory values fluctuate based on elimination probability and demand. Is a territory "safe" because many players hold it — or is it a bigger target? You can sell territories to other players at market price (3% cut applied). Buy low, sell high, survive the longest. Trading locks 5 minutes before each elimination.
5
🔍 Intel & Power-Ups
Spend FC to buy Insider Intel (25 FC) — a peek at the next elimination target before anyone else. Use Shield Tokens to protect a territory for one round. Use Fog of War (30 FC) to hide your holdings from other players' radar. Stack advantages. Outlast the competition.
6
🏆 Last Flag Takes All
When only one territory remains, all players holding that territory split the jackpot proportionally by their share. Own 3 of the 5 remaining slots in that territory? You get 60% of the entire pot. Win in crypto — smart contract pays in 60 seconds.

KEY MECHANICS

The systems that make every game different from the last.

MECHANIC_01
Proportional Winnings
Your jackpot share = your slots ÷ total slots in the winning territory. More slots = bigger cut. Buy 10 slots in the final territory and own 50% of the pool while others own fewer.
MECHANIC_02
Dynamic Market Prices
Territories near potential elimination become cheap sells and dangerous buys. Hot territories command premium prices. The market reflects collective intelligence — or collective panic.
MECHANIC_03
Shield Mechanics
A Shield Token absorbs one elimination for a territory. Max 1 per territory per round. Earn them via streaks, referrals, or buy them at 50 FC each. Use them wisely — they're the difference between surviving and losing everything.
MECHANIC_04
Insider Intel (Peek)
Pay 25 FC to see the next elimination target before the clock hits zero. Use this to sell your at-risk territory, buy a safer one, or warn your Alliance — for a price.
MECHANIC_05
5-Minute Lock Period
Trading halts 5 minutes before each elimination. This prevents last-second dumps and creates a final window of pure tension — watching your flag's fate hang on the next selection.
MECHANIC_06
Fog of War
Spend 30 FC to hide your territory holdings from the live map for one round. Other players can't see how exposed you are — or where your strengths lie. Information is power. Obscure yours.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Can I join a game that's already in progress? +
Yes. You can enter any active game at any point before the final 5 territories remain. Territories still cost 10 FC, but their relative value depends on how many rounds have passed and how many territories remain.
What happens if I hold multiple territories and one gets eliminated? +
Only the eliminated territory's slots are lost. Your other territories survive unaffected. This is why diversifying across multiple territories is a valid strategy — spread risk, maintain presence.
How long does a full game typically last? +
With 154 territories and eliminations every 60 minutes, a full game lasts approximately 6–7 days. Games with accelerated modes (Express: 30-min eliminations) last ~3 days. Special "Lightning" modes run 15-minute eliminations for weekend events.
Can I play multiple games simultaneously? +
Yes. Multiple concurrent games run at all times. You can hold positions across as many active games as your FC balance allows. Your dashboard shows all active positions and upcoming eliminations across every game.
What if I miss an elimination while offline? +
Push notifications alert you before every elimination (configurable). If you miss one and your territory is eliminated, the loss is final. This is by design — the game rewards active players. Set your alerts. Stay plugged in.
