// FREQUENTLY ASKED

GOT QUESTIONS?

What exactly is Last Flag Standing? +
Last Flag Standing is an elimination-based territory trading game played on Ethereum. Players buy flag slots in 154 countries, survive heat spikes, and outlast everyone else. The final player holding at least one flag wins the entire jackpot.
Is this gambling? +
LFS is a skill and strategy game with real monetary stakes. Understanding heat patterns, timing your trades, and managing your territory portfolio all affect your outcome. Chainlink VRF is used only for random territory selection — not for determining outcomes of strategic decisions.
What is Flag Coin (FC)? +
FC is the in-game currency used to purchase shields, fog of war, territory peeking, and other tactical moves. FC is earned through referrals, completing achievements, and as an early-access bonus. FC cannot be directly converted to ETH — it adds strategic depth to gameplay.
How long does a game last? +
Standard games run 14 days entry + 21–60 days elimination. Flash games run 7 days entry + 7–14 days elimination. Championship games are quarterly events with unlimited duration until one player remains.
How is the jackpot constructed? +
Every deposit goes directly into the smart contract vault. The winner receives 97% of the total pot. 3% goes to the house treasury for development, security, and future games. All contract logic is publicly auditable on-chain.
When do waitlist members get access? +
We're targeting a closed beta in Q1 2026 for the top 500 waitlist positions. Full public launch is planned for Q2 2026. If you're in the top 100, you'll be invited to the alpha test months before launch.
Which blockchain(s) does LFS run on? +
Launch will be on Ethereum mainnet with Polygon L2 support planned for Phase 6. Chainlink VRF provides provably fair randomness for all elimination events.
