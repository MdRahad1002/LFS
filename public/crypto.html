Provably Fair
Every elimination uses Chainlink VRF on-chain randomness. The seed is committed before the draw and revealed after. Anyone can verify any elimination in history.
📜
Smart Contracts
The jackpot pool lives in an audited Ethereum smart contract. No human can touch it. When the last flag falls, the contract auto-pays every winner in seconds.
🪙
Flag Credits (FC)
FC is the in-game token on Solana for fast, near-zero-fee transactions. Buy territories, use power-ups, trade — all on-chain. FC can be converted back to crypto anytime.
📊
Territory Trading
Territory ownership is recorded on-chain as semi-fungible tokens. The live market shows real bids and asks. Trade volume affects territory "heat" shown on the map.
🏛️
DAO Governance
Top 100 players by all-time winnings get governance tokens. Vote on new rules, new maps, fee changes, and charity donations from house profits.
⚡
Instant Withdrawals
Win and get paid in under 60 seconds. Choose BTC, ETH, SOL, USDT, or BNB. Withdrawals are gas-optimized via Solana bridge. No waiting. No paperwork.
