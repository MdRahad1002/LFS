THE HOOKS

Every feature is designed to maximize engagement, reward loyalty, and keep you coming back for the next round.

🔥

Daily Login Streaks

Log in every day to maintain your streak. Day 7 = 1 free territory. Day 30 = Shield Token. Day 100 = Legendary status with permanent 1% jackpot bonus on every win. Miss a day lose your streak. Start over.

💀

Elimination Anxiety

The countdown clock never stops. Pushes, alerts, and real-time notifications ping your phone every elimination. You'll check the app compulsively because you have to know.

🏅

Rank & Prestige System

From Recruit → Soldier → Warlord → General → Emperor. Higher ranks unlock exclusive maps, bonus multipliers, early access to new territories, and a custom animated flag border that everyone can see on the live map.

👥

Alliance System

Form alliances with up to 9 other players. When any alliance member wins, the entire alliance gets 5% of the jackpot. Share intel. Trade territories internally. Stay alive longer together.

🌊

Season Passes & Battle Pass

Every 30 days a new Season launches with a themed world map (Ancient Empires, Space Colonies, Cyberpunk Nations, etc.), exclusive NFT rewards, and a battle pass with 50 tiers of unlockables.

📣

Social Bragging & Referrals

Share your territory map on Twitter/X with one click. Refer a friend and earn 10% of their first deposit in FC. When you win, an animated victory card auto-generates for social sharing free viral marketing that pays you.
