HOW IT ALL BEGAN

In a world where borders mean everything, a secret council of global players gathers every day. They don't compete with armies they compete with flags.

Each player claims a nation. They plant their flag, back it with real crypto, and watch the clock. Because every hour, the council spins the wheel of fate and one nation falls off the map forever.

The survivors trade territories like warlords. They form alliances across continents. They buy intel on the next elimination. They hedge their bets, grow their empires, and wait hearts pounding for the clock to reach zero.

Only one flag will survive. Only one player will take the entire jackpot. Will it be yours?

⚑ The Council's Decree

"The game began in the back rooms of the internet a bet between five friends about which country would fall first. A decade later, it became the most-watched elimination event on earth."

Every game is a new world order. Old alliances crumble. New empires rise. The strongest flag doesn't always win sometimes it's the player smart enough to sell before the storm hits.

The crypto flows. The clock ticks. The map shrinks. Welcome to the last game you'll ever need.
