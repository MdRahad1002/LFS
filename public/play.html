HOW TO GET IN

From zero to first territory in under 5 minutes. No experience needed — just pick your nation and hold on.

01
CREATE YOUR ACCOUNT
Sign up with an email or connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Phantom, etc.) directly. No KYC for deposits under $2,000. Your account is your battle record — it stores rank, streak, NFTs, and history.
FREE · 30 SEC
02
DEPOSIT CRYPTO
Send BTC, ETH, SOL, USDT, or BNB to your unique deposit address. Flag Credits (FC) are credited after the first confirmation. Minimum deposit $10. First-time depositors get a +20% FC bonus.
MIN $10 · BONUS ON FIRST
03
CLAIM YOUR TERRITORY
Browse the world map. Each territory costs 10 FC. You can hold multiple territories — the more flags you plant, the broader your chance. Popular territories cost more in the secondary market. Underdogs are cheap and sometimes win.
10 FC PER TERRITORY
04
WATCH THE CLOCK & TRADE
Every 60 seconds, one nation is eliminated. Watch the map shrink in real-time. Use Insider Intel to peek at elimination odds. Deploy Shields to protect key territories. Sell before your flag gets cut — or hold and win big.
REAL-TIME · 60 SEC ROUNDS
05
WIN & WITHDRAW INSTANTLY
If you hold a surviving territory when the last flag stands, you receive a proportional share of the jackpot. Winnings hit your wallet in under 60 seconds via smart contract. No delays, no disputes, no humans in the loop.
AUTO-PAY · <60 SEC · 97% TO WINNERS
