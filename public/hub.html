THE LEGEND

In a world where borders mean everything, a secret council of global players gathers every day. They don't compete with armies they compete with flags.

Each player claims a nation. They plant their flag, back it with real crypto, and watch the clock. Because every hour, the council spins the wheel of fate and one nation falls off the map forever.

The survivors trade territories like warlords. They form alliances across continents. They buy intel on the next elimination. They hedge their bets, grow their empires, and wait hearts pounding for the clock to reach zero.

Only one flag will survive. Only one player will take the entire jackpot. Will it be yours?

⚑ The Council's Decree

"The game began in the back rooms of the internet a bet between five friends about which country would fall first. A decade later, it became the most-watched elimination event on earth."

Every game is a new world order. Old alliances crumble. New empires rise. The strongest flag doesn't always win sometimes it's the player smart enough to sell before the storm hits.

The crypto flows. The clock ticks. The map shrinks. Welcome to the last game you'll ever need.

HOW IT WORKS

Six steps from zero to jackpot. Simple to learn, impossible to stop playing.

PHASE 01
Entry Window
Deposit crypto and buy territory slots for 10 FC each. No entry limit.
PHASE 02
Round Opens
60-minute clock starts. Trading opens. Intel can be purchased.
PHASE 03
Lock Window
All trades freeze 5 minutes before elimination. Hold your breath.
PHASE 04
Elimination
Chainlink VRF fires. One territory falls. The jackpot grows.
PHASE 05
Repeat
New round begins. Survivors continue. New players can buy in.
FINAL
Last Flag
One territory remains. All holders split 97% of the jackpot in 60 seconds.
1
💰 Deposit Crypto
Fund your account using BTCETHSOLUSDTBNB. Your crypto is converted into Flag Credits (FC) the in-game currency. 1 FC = $1 USD equivalent. First deposit gets a +20% bonus.
2
🌍 Claim Your Territory
Browse the live world map. Each territory costs 10 FC to enter. You can own multiple territories the more you own, the higher your jackpot share. Each territory you buy adds 9.7 FC to the prize pool (3% house cut).
3
⏱️ The Elimination Clock
Every 60 minutes, the system selects one territory for elimination using a provably fair on-chain random function. All players holding that territory are knocked out. The map shrinks. The jackpot grows. Tension builds.
4
📈 Trade the Market
Territory values fluctuate based on elimination probability. If a territory has many players, it's "safer" by volume or a bigger target? You can sell territories to other players at market price (3% cut). Buy low, sell high, survive.
5
🔍 Intel & Power-Ups
Spend FC to buy Insider Intel a peek at the next elimination target. Use Shield Tokens (earnable via streaks) to protect a territory for one round. Use Fog of War to hide your holdings from other players' radar.
6
🏆 Last Flag Takes All
When only one territory remains, all players holding that territory split the jackpot proportionally by their share. Own 3 of the 5 remaining slots in that territory? You get 60% of the entire pot. Win in crypto withdraw instantly.

THE RULES

Clear, fair, unbreakable. Every rule is enforced by smart contract no human interference possible.

🎯
Fair Elimination
Eliminations are determined by a Chainlink VRF (Verifiable Random Function). The result is provably random, publicly auditable, and impossible to manipulate not even by us.
💎
Entry & Buy-In
Each territory slot costs 10 FC. Each territory has unlimited slots. Multiple players can hold the same country they split any winnings proportionally if that territory is the last one.
☠️
No Refunds on Elimination
When your territory is eliminated, those credits are gone. This is the game. However you can sell before elimination using the live market. Sell fast. Sell smart.
🔄
Trading Rules
Territories can be traded peer-to-peer at any price you set. The house takes a 3% cut on every trade. You cannot trade after the countdown reaches 5 minutes (lock period).
🛡️
Shield Tokens
Shields can be earned through daily streaks, referrals, or purchased. One Shield protects one territory for one elimination round. Max 1 Shield per territory per round. Shields cannot be stacked.
🏆
Jackpot Distribution
The final jackpot is 97% of total pool. 3% is the permanent house cut. Winners receive payout in their preferred crypto within 60 seconds of game end, via smart contract auto-execution.
